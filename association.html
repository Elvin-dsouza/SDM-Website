Admissions

Eligibility Criteria

BBA, BCA, B.Com and BA(HRD) Pass in the 2 - years pre-university Examination conducted by the Karnataka PUE board or any other examination considered equivalent by the Mangalore University.

Application and Requirements

The application and prospectus for the above courses can be taken from the college office immediately after the announcement of second PUC results. Filled up application should be submitted to the office along with the following within a week.

  • Registration fee - non refundable
  • Attested copies of:
  • PUC Marks Card
  • Conduct certificate
  • Caste certificate if any
  • Passport size photograph
  • Proof of date of birth / SSLC marks card with date of birth

Interview

Merit list of the candidate short listed for the interview will be displayed on the notice board along with the interview date. Candidates called for an interview shall be accompanied by their parents. Following original certificates shall be produced during the interview.

  • Conduct certificate
  • Transfer certificate
  • Secondary school leaving certificate
  • PUC marks card
  • Caste certificate / income certificate for claiming Fees Concession if any.
  • Eligibility certificate if the candidate is coming from other Institute / Board.
  • Any other Certificate necessary for admission
  • Passport size photograph- 4 copies.
